5 Turtle Facts

Neat Facts About Turtles

  • Turtles are the one of the oldest reptile groups(over 200 million years!).
  • A turtles shell is part of their body, which means that they cannot come out of it.
  • A turtles environment determines their diet, some are even carnivores!
  • Even though many turtles live in the water, they breathe air and lay eggs on land.
  • Many turtle species are endangered, but there are many turtle sanctuaries worldwide!
